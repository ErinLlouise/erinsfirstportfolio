Bootcamp learning plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

Post EDA graduation I want to find a job with a company that I love and work with a team of great people for a few years while I continue to develope my skills. I am also considering doing freelance webdevelopment for small busniesses or individuals but I am not too sure yet.





What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I think a strength I can bring to bootcamp is personal time management. Considering my realisations in my final reflection, I believe I can and will manage my time wisely when working individually and in a team.
Two distinctive limitations come to mind. First being public speeking. In bootcamp, I believe we are required to do live coding presentations to our kohort which I the last thing I want to do - I absolutely hate presenting myself or my work in front of more than a few people.
Other limitation is tiredness. I have chronic fatigue which can sometimes impact my work when I don't give my brain rest - I will be trying my very best to avoid this.





What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

I do really enjoy talking and getting to know to people and generally don't have much social shyness (unless its public speaking - obviously). But I suppose this will be the first time in my life where I spend lots of time with people who are not my own age and are most likely more mature and wiser than myself. I suppose one of my worries is that I may struggle to connect with some people but with the human skills development, I don't think it will be a problem!





What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

Definitely public speaking and presenting myself to a group of people.





What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

From what I have experienced so far, EDA staff has been more than accomodating and helpful when it comes to the tech skills so I suppose I would expect the same kind of helpfulness with human skills.





What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

One thing that I really want to be able to accomplish is practicing meditation. I find it helpful and relaxing when I do it - which is about once a month. So I want to be able to get into a habit and find the right kind of meditation that works well for me.
